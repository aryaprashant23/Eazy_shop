# Project Context: Eazy Shop (Virtual POS)

## Overview
Eazy Shop (Pay & Go) is a Virtual Point of Sale (POS) platform designed to eliminate billing queues in traditional supermarkets by transforming a customer's smartphone into a self-service checkout system.

## The Problem
Traditional supermarket checkouts rely entirely on physical POS counters operated by cashiers. This centralized billing model results in:
- **For Customers**: Long waiting times in queues, frustration, and a poor shopping experience, often leading to cart abandonment.
- **For Supermarkets**: Checkout bottlenecks during peak hours, limited cashier capacity, lost sales, and higher operational costs.
- **For Brands & Malls**: Lost purchases and reduced time spent exploring other stores.

## The Solution
Instead of increasing physical billing counters, Eazy Shop moves the POS directly to the customer's smartphone. The mobile app handles:
- Barcode scanning via camera
- Product identification and pricing
- Smart cart management
- Digital payments (UPI, Cards, Wallets)
- Digital receipt generation

## Core User Journey
1. **Check-in**: Customer opens the app and selects the store.
2. **Shop & Scan**: Customer picks items and scans their barcodes using the phone.
3. **Smart Cart**: Scanned products are instantly added to the digital cart with live running totals.
4. **Pay & Go**: Customer completes payment digitally within the app.
5. **Exit**: Customer shows a generated Exit QR Code and digital receipt to staff, bypassing the traditional queue entirely.

## Prototype Scope (MVP)
The current MVP focuses purely on the software aspects of this flow:
- **Frontend App**: A simulated mobile UI for customers to scan, manage cart, and pay.
- **Frontend Admin**: A dashboard for store managers to track live sessions, inventory, and verify exits.
- **Backend**: A central server managing product data, order transactions, and stock updates.

## Key Differentiator
Unlike traditional shopping apps for delivery, Eazy Shop focuses on the **in-store** physical shopping experience, marrying the convenience of digital checkout with immediate physical product possession.
